Corporate Design

Corporate identity is only one aspect of corporate branding, specifically the company's visible image. As a result, corporate identity is likely to be most visible through corporate design. The company logo, its visual corporate presence at trade shows, the design of the company website, and the outstanding design features of company merchandise and packaging are all examples of corporate design.
